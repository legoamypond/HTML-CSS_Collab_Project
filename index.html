Saftey in Archery

DO NOT SKIP THIS SECTION!!! I know its quite suprising, but bows and arrows are quite dangerous. The bow alone is very dangerous if you dry fire. Which is shooting a bow without an arrow. Not only does dry firing put you in danger, it also damages the bow. When handeling arrows it important to keep on hand on the tip and the other on the shaft. You know, so you don't take an eye out. Its also common to get bruises on your arm after firing. Sometimes arm gaurds can help. But the most effective way to avoid getting bruises is to bend your arm away from the string. When you are at an archery range you will also have to respect specific procedures and commands they have there. For example, they will give you commands indicating that you can fire. Good Job!!!
